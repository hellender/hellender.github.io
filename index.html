Bounding Hare


Chapter I: Crossing

Jun 16th, 2014

The cultivated blades sheltered me as I began my last dance. My partner watched across the way. I skipped into a collision, ricocheted across and collapsed. There, I laid on my side, crushed. I was leaving myself for dead.
